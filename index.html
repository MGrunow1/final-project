Michael's Nature Page

I have grown up taking walks, looking at scenery and listening to the sounds of birds. However, I do not have to travel far to see a bird on a fence, or to look at the shape of a cloud (at least, when the sky has clouds). I still prefer to get out to a park, where I can photograph the landscape.

I love to learn to identify the plants and animals that I see.
