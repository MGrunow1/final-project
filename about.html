About Michael

I love to identify the plants and animals that I see, and continue to learn to recognize more. I may take along multiple books for identifying things (trees, beetles, spiders), as well as binoculars.

I enjoy photography. Mostly, I take pictures of landscapes, but I will also take photos of flowers or mushrooms, or anything close enough for me to get a decent picture. Sometimes I have videoed a walk, and recorded sounds. All the sound recordings on this website are taken out of videos that I recorded.
